WHO WE ARE

Built on Belief.

Ripple is a volunteer-led organisation providing free tutoring to students with disabilities or learning conditions. We believe education should be inclusive, supportive, and accessible — without cost being a barrier.

OUR MISSION

That's how confidence grows. That's how futures change.

By matching students with trained, compassionate volunteers, we create learning spaces where students feel understood, supported, and capable.

Our goal isn't just academic improvement — it's confidence, independence, and belief. Belief that every student, regardless of their challenges, has the capacity to grow.

WHAT DRIVES US

Our Values

01

Inclusion First

Every student, regardless of disability or learning condition, deserves access to quality education support without financial barriers.

02

Genuine Connection

We believe the most powerful learning happens when a student feels truly understood and supported by someone who cares.

03

Lasting Impact

Our work goes beyond grades. We aim to build confidence, independence, and belief in each student's own potential.
